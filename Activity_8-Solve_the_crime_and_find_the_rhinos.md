#### **Activity 8: Solve the crime and find the rhinos**

**The Activity:** In New Orleans, they have made it illegal to have more than 10 unique images of rhinoceroses and a PhD student is suspected of breaking this law. Can you find out if the suspect is guilty or not?

**Why it's fun:** This is a simulation of a real world digital investigation letting you use the tools the real investigators use.

**How to do it:**

1. Download and install a copy of [Oracle VirtualBox](https://www.virtualbox.org/).
2. Download the [challenge VM](https://globalbanking-my.sharepoint.com/:u:/g/personal/fmitchell_globalbanking_ac_uk/EWIPb3M9i2lOqpC4vz1VtPMBFGTsqyzTX3eLG1iET67yog?e=aurcdg) and load it into VirtualBox
3. Follow the instructions on the [Challenge Sheet](Support_Files/Roedeo_Challenge_GBS.pdf).
