#### **Activity 13: Protecting the startup from cyber attacks**

**The Activity:** You have been given a job at an exciting new startup, in charge of their cyber security. Can you help the company grow without your servers being compromised?

**Why it's fun:** This simulation will give an insight into what it's like being a CISO (Chief Information Security Officer) but without requiring a large amount of technical skills.
