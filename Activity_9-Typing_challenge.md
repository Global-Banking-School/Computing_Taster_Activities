#### **Activity 9: Typing challenge**

**The Activity:** Improve your typing skills by defending your planet from aliens.

**Why it's fun:** Having fast and accurate typing skills will make your life easier as computer scientists, but traditional typing trainers are boring. This is a fun way to improve your typing skills as part of a game.
